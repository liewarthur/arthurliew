01 / About

Arthur is a Kuala Lumpur-based film director and educator — where technical craft meets cinematic instinct.

Founder of production house Blackdotpeople, his work spans commercial campaigns to short films. A member of FDAM and PROFIMA, rooted in Malaysia's creative industry.

Most recently an alumnus of the 2025 BIFAN NAFF Fantastic Film School, Arthur directs, lectures, and explores the frontier of AI-driven visual storytelling — always evolving, always story-first.
