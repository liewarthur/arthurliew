01 / Profile

Arthur is a Kuala Lumpur-based film director and educator — where technical rigour meets cinematic instinct.

Co-founder of production house Blackdotpeople, his work spans commercial campaigns to short films. A member of FDAM and PROFIMA, rooted in Malaysia's creative industry.

He directs, lectures, and explores the frontier of AI-driven visual storytelling — always evolving, always story-first.

《血債》BLOOD DEBT

許願時，必有債。 血債，血償。 一個走投無路的男人踏入森林，以命換願——卻不知他將以血償還這筆血債。

When you wish, there is a debt. Blood debt, blood pays. A man with nowhere left to turn enters the forest to bargain — offering his life for a wish, not knowing the blood debt he must repay.
