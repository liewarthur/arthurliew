01 / About

Arthur is a Kuala Lumpur-based film director and educator — where technical craft meets cinematic instinct.

Founder of production house Blackdotpeople, his work spans commercial campaigns to short films. A member of FDAM and PROFIMA, rooted in Malaysia's creative industry.

Most recently an alumnus of the 2025 BIFAN NAFF Fantastic Film School, Arthur directs, lectures, and explores the frontier of AI-driven visual storytelling — always evolving, always story-first.

《血債》BLOOD DEBT

許願時，必有債。 血債，血償。 一個走投無路的男人踏入森林，以命換願——卻不知他將以血償還這筆血債。

When you wish, there is a debt. Blood debt, blood pays. A man with nowhere left to turn enters the forest to bargain — offering his life for a wish, not knowing the blood debt he must repay.

Feature Film - In Development

《念》PAPER GIRL

What we cannot say to the living, we burn for the dead. Grief is a loop. Love is the exit.

Paper Girl is a silent feature about a paper offering sent into the underworld carrying enough love to free a mother who can no longer leave on her own.

It is about guilt that cannot be spoken to the living, and what love costs when it tries to cross the gate of death. The dead are not stuck in punishment. They are stuck in the last emotional state someone felt for them.
